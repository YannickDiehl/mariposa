================================================================
STATISTICAL NOTES
================================================================

T-TEST Statistics Computed:
1. Levene\
2. T-statistic (both equal and unequal variance assumptions)
3. Degrees of freedom
4. Significance (2-tailed p-value)
5. Mean difference
6. Standard error of difference
7. Confidence intervals of difference

Effect Size Formulas (to be calculated from group statistics):
Cohen\
Pooled_SD = sqrt(((n1-1)*SD1^2 + (n2-1)*SD2^2) / (n1+n2-2))
Hedges\
Glass\

For weighted analyses:
- Weighted means and SDs are used
- Effective sample size = (sum of weights)^2 / sum(weights^2)
